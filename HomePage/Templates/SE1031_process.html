The 1031 Exchange Process

A successful 1031 exchange requires more than meeting IRS deadlines — it demands protecting capital at every step. SimpleEXCHANGE 1031 coordinates the entire process through the SimpleCITI platform, ensuring structural compliance, operational execution, and fiduciary oversight from initiation through completion.

1

Initial Consultation & Strategy Design

  • Comprehensive assessment of investor goals, property profile, and tax optimization objectives
  • SimpleADVISORY defines governance requirements and confirms regulatory compliance framework
  • SimpleCITI Structuring Team outlines DST, TIC, UPREIT, or hybrid structuring options
2

Sale of Relinquished Property

  • Investor completes property sale with proceeds held by qualified Qualified Intermediary (QI)
  • SimpleADVISORY ensures full compliance with IRS exchange requirements and documentation
  • SimpleBROKER may provide marketing assistance and sale value optimization strategies
3

45-Day Identification Period

  • Identify qualified replacement properties within strict IRS deadlines and documentation requirements
  • SimpleBROKER sources and evaluates replacement opportunities meeting investment criteria
  • SimpleADVISORY validates compliance standards and conducts risk assessment analysis
4

180-Day Acquisition & Closing

  • Acquire replacement property with appropriate structuring into DST, TIC, or UPREIT vehicle
  • SimpleSECURITIES / SimpleEXCHANGE manages structuring details and investor onboarding process
  • SimpleADVISORY oversees documentation, compliance reporting, and regulatory requirements
5

Post-Closing Operations

  • Ongoing ownership management and performance oversight with regular monitoring
  • SimpleMANAGE handles tenant relations, NOI optimization, and day-to-day operations
  • SimpleBRICKS executes capital improvements, renovations, and value-enhancement projects
  • SimpleBROKER drives leasing strategy, tenant optimization, and market positioning
6

Ongoing Oversight & Reporting

  • Continuous compliance monitoring and comprehensive investor communication
  • SimpleADVISORY provides governance oversight, independent audits, and performance transparency
7

Liquidity & Exit Strategy

  • Properties may be sold, refinanced, or contributed into UPREIT structure based on market conditions
  • SimpleBROKER manages sale positioning, marketing execution, and transaction optimization
  • SimpleADVISORY validates distribution calculations, tax reporting, and compliance documentation
