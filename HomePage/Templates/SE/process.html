DST Handcuffs™

Under IRS Revenue Ruling 2004-86-DSTs are prohibited from refinancing or modifying loans, even in critical situations — handcuffing both the trust and its investors. Many DSTs are compelled to 721 UPREIT conversions to avoid default, rolling investors into another company's structure with up to a six-month stock lock-up and limited liquidity — often difficult to sell. No decisions. Refinance risk. Low liquidity. Caged in.
