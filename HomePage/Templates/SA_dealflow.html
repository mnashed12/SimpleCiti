Our 6-Step Process

From discovery to optimization, we provide institutional-grade governance at every stage

1

Discovery & Alignment

Understand the partner's strategy, target investors, and operational infrastructure. Identify governance, compliance, and reporting requirements for capital readiness.
2

Governance Framework Design

Build or adapt frameworks to meet regulatory standards and institutional expectations. Define approvals, cadences, protocols, and authorities.
3

Regulatory & Operational Integration

Implement monitoring systems, compliance workflows, and investor communication processes.
4

Oversight in Action

Actively review metrics, risks, and compliance milestones; coordinate directly with operators and asset managers.
5

Continuous Reporting & Audit Readiness

Maintain investor and regulatory reporting in audit-ready formats.
6

Post-Investment Review & Optimization

Conduct governance post-mortems to strengthen future engagements.

Partnership Model

SimpleADVISORY works with partners in multiple configurations — from full platform governance to deal-by-deal oversight. Our partnership model is designed to provide execution capabilities, capital alignment, and regulatory credibility.
1

Co-Sponsored Investment Vehicles

Provide institutional governance for vehicles jointly launched with SimpleCITI or external partners. Apply consistent oversight processes — due diligence, monitoring, investor reporting — to every co-sponsored transaction. Commit 5–25% GP capital alongside partners, filling capital gaps and accelerating time to close without compromising diligence.
2

Platform Joint Ventures

Serve as the governance and compliance function for large-scale JV structures between operating companies and capital providers. Coordinate between sponsor and investor reporting requirements, ensuring consistency and transparency. Oversee governance provisions within operating agreements to protect all stakeholders.
3

Oversight-Only Engagements

Provide independent oversight of a single deal, fund, or portfolio for LPs or co-investors. Deliver full reporting packages and compliance verification. Identify operational or governance gaps before they become material risks.
