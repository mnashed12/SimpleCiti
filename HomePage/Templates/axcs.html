SimpleAXCS

Strategic Passive Investments with Lasting Impact

SimpleAXCS is the dedicated investment arm of a multi-generational family office, specializing in passive, long-horizon capital allocations. Our platform focuses on three core verticals — Venture Capital, Private Equity, and Hedge Funds — with the objective of achieving sustainable, risk-adjusted returns through a disciplined and research-driven process.

We believe that long-term capital deserves long-term thinking. That’s why our passive investment model emphasizes access over control, allowing top-tier managers and sponsors to focus on alpha generation while we ensure structural integrity, transparency, and alignment of interests.

What We Do

Venture Capital

We partner with leading venture capital firms and syndicates targeting early- and growth-stage startups with transformative potential. Our portfolio includes exposure to emerging technologies, SaaS, biotech, fintech, and climate innovation.

Private Equity

Our PE exposure includes mid-market buyouts, growth equity, and secondaries through trusted sponsor relationships. We emphasize platform scalability, EBITDA visibility, and strong operator DNA in every deal.

Hedge Funds

We allocate to institutional-quality hedge funds across multi-strategy, global macro, and long/short equity. Our selection process prioritizes manager discipline, risk management infrastructure, and return consistency over time.

Our Investment Philosophy

  • Passive with Purpose: We believe in giving capital to the right hands and letting it work. Our strategy minimizes active intervention while maintaining oversight through governance rights, detailed reporting, and robust LP agreements.
  • Manager Selection: We conduct thorough due diligence on every GP and vehicle, focusing on process repeatability, historical performance, LP treatment, and incentive alignment.
  • Portfolio Construction: Our allocations are diversified by strategy, vintage, geography, and liquidity profile. We maintain a long-term outlook, balancing illiquid commitments with tactical, opportunistic liquidity.
  • Family Office Values: As principals, we optimize for wealth preservation, intergenerational growth, and ethical stewardship—not short-term exits.

Whether you're a founder seeking committed capital, a fund manager looking for a long-term LP, or a co-investor interested in thoughtful capital partnerships, SimpleAXCS operates with clarity, discretion, and generational vision.
