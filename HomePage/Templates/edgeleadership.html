SHERVIN ZADE

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Shervin oversees a network of 12 operating companies with a focus on real estate, advisory, and asset management. With 30+ years of experience in real estate acquisition, development, and operations, he combines strategic vision with expertise in regulatory compliance and innovative investment strategies. As founder of USN Corp, a vertically integrated consumer goods company, Shervin scaled the business to 22 North American facilities, employing 1,700 workers and managing key mergers and acquisitions. He spearheaded an FDA-compliant facility for Johnson & Johnson, securing an $800-million contract. Shervin is also an NYU Stern graduate and resident of Long Island, Shervin is also a dedicated philanthropist and board member.

Rody leads the strategic growth of the Specialty Finance division, overseeing five operating companies.
