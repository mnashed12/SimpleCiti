Specialty Lending Solutions

Explore our suite of tailored capital solutions designed to meet unique financing needs across a range of asset types and business goals.

Residential Transition Loans (RTL)

Designed for stabilized rental properties producing consistent income. Suitable for long-term hold strategies across SFR, multifamily, or mixed-use assets.

  • Cash-out options available
  • Fixed and adjustable rates

Commercial Bridge Loans (CBL)

Short-term financing for acquisitions, repositioning, or time-sensitive closings. Designed to bridge gaps until permanent funding is secured.

  • Close in as little as 7 days
  • Up to 80% LTV
  • Terms from 6–24 months

Preferred Equity

A flexible capital layer that complements senior debt. Ideal for developers and sponsors looking to maximize leverage while retaining ownership.

  • Enhances returns without dilution
  • Structured repayment terms
  • Used for acquisition or recapitalization

Equipment & Leasing

Provides capital to acquire or lease equipment for business operations. Ideal for construction, logistics, and healthcare sectors.

  • Terms from 12–60 months
  • Up to 100% financing
  • Quick approvals and delivery

Merchant Cash Advance (MCA)

Fast and flexible financing for businesses with strong daily revenue. Ideal for short-term needs and working capital with repayment tied to future sales.

  • Approval within 24–48 hours
  • Daily or weekly repayment schedules
  • No collateral required
