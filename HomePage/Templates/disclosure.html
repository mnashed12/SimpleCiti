    Transparency and Disclosure Policy

    SimpleCiti Companies (“SimpleCiti,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency and regulatory compliance. This Transparency and Disclosure Policy is designed to provide clear and comprehensive information to our investors, clients, and stakeholders, ensuring compliance with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”) regulations, federal securities laws, and applicable industry best practices.

    1. Investment Disclosures and Offering Documents

    All investment opportunities offered by SimpleCiti are made exclusively through formal offering materials, including Private Placement Memoranda (“PPM”), Subscription Agreements, and other legal documents. These documents contain detailed disclosures about the investment objectives, risks, fees, conflicts of interest, and financial statements.

    Investors must review these materials carefully and consult with their legal, tax, and financial advisors before making any investment decision.

    2. Risk Disclosures

    Investments in private equity, real estate, specialty finance, and other alternative asset classes involve significant risks, including but not limited to:

    • Loss of principal investment
    • Illiquidity and long investment horizons
    • Market, economic, and regulatory risks
    • Dependence on management and operational risks
    • No guarantee of returns or performance

    Past performance is not indicative of future results. Prospective investors should fully understand these risks before committing capital.

    3. Conflicts of Interest

    SimpleCiti and its affiliates may engage in transactions or hold interests that could present potential conflicts of interest. We are committed to identifying, disclosing, and managing such conflicts in accordance with applicable law and industry best practices.

    Where conflicts arise, SimpleCiti will disclose these conflicts to investors in offering documents and seek to ensure that all parties are treated fairly and equitably.

    4. Accredited Investor Status

    All investments offered by SimpleCiti are available only to accredited investors as defined by SEC Regulation D. By investing, you represent that you meet the criteria of an accredited investor. We may request verification documentation to confirm your status.

    5. Privacy and Data Protection

    SimpleCiti respects your privacy and complies with all applicable data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable. We collect, use, and safeguard personal data responsibly and disclose how your information is used in our Privacy Policy.

    6. Use of Third-Party Service Providers

    We may use third-party service providers to support our business operations and investment activities. These providers are required to comply with all relevant confidentiality and data security standards.

    7. Reporting and Communication

    SimpleCiti provides investors with regular reports, updates, and communications consistent with legal and regulatory requirements. We are committed to timely and accurate disclosure of material information affecting your investments.

    8. Amendments to this Policy

    We reserve the right to amend this Transparency and Disclosure Policy at any time to comply with regulatory changes or business needs. Updated versions will be posted on our website with the effective date clearly indicated.
