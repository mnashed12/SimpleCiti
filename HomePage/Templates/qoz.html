About SimpleQOZ

SimpleQOZ specializes in Qualified Opportunity Zone (QOZ) investments, offering family offices direct access to high-impact real estate and business ventures within economically underserved communities. Through the strategic use of federal tax incentives, we empower our clients to defer, reduce, and potentially eliminate capital gains, while fostering long-term economic growth and social revitalization. Our approach blends sophisticated tax planning with purpose-driven investing-delivering both measurable returns and meaningful impact.

Who We Serve: Families Like Ours

We work best with entrepreneurial families, real asset builders, and founders navigating complexity post-liquidity. Our clients typically hold $50M–$1B in net worth and seek control, clarity, and complete alignment—without sales pitches or product agendas.
