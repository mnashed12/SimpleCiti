SimpleCITI is a vertically integrated real estate and capital markets platform built for the next generation of private markets. This fully integrated approach allows us to optimize every phase of the investment and asset lifecycle while providing a seamless experience for partners, investors, and tenants alike.

SimpleCITI Companies

SimpleCITI's vertically integrated approach to real estate investing and asset management leverages strong operational capabilities at each stage of the transactions to seek attractive risk-adjusted returns. Our vertically integrated structure allows us to capitalize on operational synergies by facilitating both arms-length and non-arms-length transactions between business units. While certain transactions will be conducted at market terms to ensure fairness, others may deviate to optimize efficiency and value creation, with full transparency and oversight to address protential conflicts of interest.

  • COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES: With comprehensive in-house expertise in property accounting, leasing, and development, SimpleCITI is equipped to drive value creation while minimizing risk exposure.
  • SOURCING ABILITIES: SimpleCITI operates as both an owner and operator, leveraging a broad network of relationships with leasing brokers, tenants, and industry peers. Our acquisitions team brings deep expertise in industrial real estate, having previously run a global industrial business and collaborated with leading e-commerce companies.
  • BESPOKE MARKET ACCESS: All assets have historically been acquired in non-competitive situations.
  • ASSET SELECTION: SimpleCITI generally invests in assets of a smaller size than those pursued by competing investment managers, funds, or REIT's, reducing competition.
  • OPERATING EFFICIENCIES: SimpleCITI's integrated property-level accounting, leasing, and development capabilities drive cost efficiency and boost profitability. This cohesive approach allows for real-time asset management and effective risk mitigation.
