Top 10 Questions to Ask Before Hiring Your Private Fund Advisor or Asset Manager

Choosing the right Private Fund Advisor (PFA) or Asset Manager (AM) is mission-critical. Ask these questions to find a partner who drives results across real estate, private credit, litigation finance, AI/data centers, and securities investments.

  1. What is your strategy to turn my capital into measurable value?
    At SimpleADVISORY, we build detailed business plans for each asset or fund, specifying operational, structural, and capital levers to drive targeted returns while preserving downside protection.
  2. How do you measure success across different investments?
    We track IRR, equity multiple, yield on cost, DSCR, litigation win rates, compute ROI, tax-adjusted returns, and other key metrics to inform proactive decisions.
  3. How do you identify and manage risks before they affect performance?
    We perform sector-specific risk assessments pre- and post-investment, integrating scenario analysis and active monitoring to mitigate legal, operational, market, and counterparty risks early.
  4. How do you tailor your approach to each client's goals and constraints?
    We begin with deep understanding of your objectives and timelines, then customize investment structures, strategies, and reporting to align with your goals.
  5. How do you manage and coordinate the specialists needed to execute your strategy?
    We lead and integrate brokers, underwriters, engineers, attorneys, operators, and servicers, ensuring all parties are accountable to your investment outcomes.
  6. How do you integrate capital markets insights into your asset or fund decisions?
    We continuously evaluate refinancing, recapitalization, co-investment opportunities, securitizations, and exits to enhance returns and liquidity across market cycles.
  7. How do you create operational alpha?
    We drive value through initiatives like lease restructuring, densification, optimal loan structuring, litigation claim prioritization, and AI/data power optimization, tailored by asset class.
  8. How do you remain agile to pivot when markets shift?
    Our investment committees monitor market signals in real time to reallocate capital, adjust underwriting, or reposition strategies swiftly and strategically.
  9. What is your decision-making process for holding, selling, refinancing, or redeploying capital?
    We evaluate hold, sell, refinance, or redeploy options based on multi-factor analysis of current performance, market trends, capital costs, and your evolving goals.
  10. How do you ensure your interests are aligned with mine?
    We operate with fiduciary duty, full transparency, and alignment structures including performance-based promotes and co-investments to prioritize your outcomes above all.
