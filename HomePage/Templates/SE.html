Structural Provisions Within Product Offerings

Capital Call Provisions: In the Investor Operating Agreement, give the Sponsor LLC the right to make capital calls if additional funding is needed, with investors able to contribute or face dilution.

Preferred Return and Waterfall Structure: Structure a preferred return in the Investor Operating Agreement (e.g., 8%) before profits split with a promote to the Sponsor

Exit Rights and Drag-Along/Tag-Along Clauses: Consider drag-along rights to initiate a full sale, with tag-along rights for investors.

Claw back Provision: Add a claw back if project returns fall below target, ensuring fair distributions.

Key Person Clause: Establish a transition plan if a key sponsor member is unable to continue, so the project isn’t disrupted.
