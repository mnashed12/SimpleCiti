About Fort Morgan Capital

Institutional-Grade Litigation Finance

Specialized investment firm focused on litigation finance and alternative investments, targeting high-yield opportunities with asymmetric risk profiles.

Fort Morgan Capital (FMC) provides $2M-$15M in tailored liquidity and growth capital to law firms, offering financial solutions designed to support firms at every stage of litigation. FMC delivers institutional-grade funding with the flexibility and responsiveness law firms need to succeed.

With a decade of litigation finance expertise, FMC works with single-case and mass tort firms to provide capital for strategic marketing initiatives, operational efficiencies, debt refinancing, and legal asset structuring. By advancing funds against pending invoices, FMC helps firms improve cash flow stability, ensuring they can focus on advocacy without financial constraints. More than just a capital provider, FMC partners with law firms to navigate the financial complexities of litigation, offering the resources and expertise needed to scale operations, optimize case outcomes, and maximize long-term growth.
