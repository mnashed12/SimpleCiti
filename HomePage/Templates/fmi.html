Fort Morgan Injury Law

Where Legal Power Meets Investment Potential

Fort Morgan Injury Law, powered by Citi Injury Law, breaks new ground by enabling non-lawyers to invest in one of the most lucrative legal sectors — personal injury and mass tort litigation.

A Unique Opportunity for Non-Lawyers in Personal Injury and Mass Tort Litigation. Citi Injury Law offers a groundbreaking investment opportunity in the multi billion-dollar personal injury and mass tort litigation market. As an Alternative Business Structure (ABS), we allow non-lawyers to invest in a proven legal practice, creating a bridge between legal expertise and investment potential. Our firm focuses on single-event personal injury cases (Motor Vehicle Accidents, Slip & Fall, etc.) as well as mass tort litigation (Roundup, J&J Baby Powder, and more).

  • ✔ Single-event injury cases (Motor Vehicle, Slip & Fall, etc.)
  • ✔ Mass tort opportunities (e.g., Roundup, J&J Baby Powder)
  • ✔ Strategic legal settlements designed to maximize returns
  • ✔ Hands-off investor experience backed by legal experts
