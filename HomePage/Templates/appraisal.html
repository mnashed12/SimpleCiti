About

SimpleAPPRAISALS is a leader in the valuation and appraisal industry, with a team of diverse professionals experienced in a wide range of real estate valuation disciplines. Our services are relied upon to assist with domestic transactions, ensuring accurate property valuations and providing assessments of ROI for optimal investment gains. We specialize in acquisitions and recapitalizations, due diligence services, investment strategy, portfolio analysis, and property inspections, all aimed at supporting clients in making informed and effective real estate decisions.

With over two decades of delivering exceptional client experiences, our team has appraised thousands of properties for personal, legal, and financial matters, accumulating tens of billions in property valuations. We have exclusive expertise in appraising various property types, including fee simple, leased fee, and leasehold interests, as well as determining market value and market rental value. Our team members have also served as expert witnesses casses across 18 states.

2. Report Type (Services)

A. Inspections

  • Drive-By: Exterior-only property review.
  • Pre-Purchase: Detailed inspection before property acquisition.
  • Condition Report: Evaluation of property condition and repair costs.
  • Full Appraisal: Comprehensive assessment, covering interior/exterior features.
  • Bifurcated: Remote analysis and completion by the appraiser, using third party data.
  • Post-Disaster: Assessment of damage and property condition after natural disasters.

B. Appraisals

  • Retrospective: Determines property value as of a specific past date.
  • Litigation & Estate: Disputes, inheritance, probate, or estate planning.
  • Access Rights: Valuations for infrastructure, easements, or public acquisitions.
  • Commercial: Accurate valuation for offices, retail, residential, industrial & mixed use.
  • Land & Development: Specialized assessment of vacant land or development value.
  • Insurance/Replacement: Calculates rebuild or replacement cost for insurance policies.
  • Investment & Portfolio: Evaluates income potential for individual assets or portfolios.

C. Valuations

  • Liquidation/Quick-Sale Value: Establishes pricing for rapid sale scenarios.
  • Desktop Valuation: Remote assessment using public records & third-party.
  • Market Rent & Tax: Provides rental income potential or property tax estimates.
  • Market Valuation: Determines fair market value under current market conditions.
  • Highest & Best Use Analysis: Evaluates the optimal legal, profitable use of a property.
  • Development Feasibility: Analyzes financial & practical vaiability of development projects.
  • As-Is & After-Repair Value: Estimates current property value & post-renovation potential.
